Are Flowers A Good Birthday Gift?

Flowers are a popular gift for a variety of occasions. From anniversaries and thanking someone to graduations and funerals. But, are flowers a good birthday gift? Absolutely! Everyone loves gifting (and receiving) blooms as they’re stunning and meaningful. However, choosing the right birthday flowers can be difficult – that’s why we’re here to help! Read on to find out why flowers are such a special gift and how to pick the best ones.

Are flowers a good birthday gift?

Gift giving can be difficult, especially when you’re unsure what to get your loved one. That’s why flower bouquets make a great gift every time. They’re beautiful, mood-lifting and great for people who have limited space. Flowers are also a good birthday gift because they can be delivered right to someone’s doorstep!

Why do we give flowers on birthdays?

Flowers have been a go-to birthday gift for centuries. There are many reasons why people love to give flowers as birthday gifts, such as:

  • Lifting someone’s mood: There’s no better feeling than receiving a beautiful and bright flower arrangement. No matter the occasion, flowers are always appreciated because it means someone special is thinking of you.
  • Communicating emotions: Each type of flower is capable of expressing different emotions to someone special. Whether these are emotions of love, joy, sympathy or appreciation, flowers will always connect with positive emotions.
  • Convenient: With so many different options to choose from, flowers are one of the most convenient gifts around. They are easily customisable by colour and size to suit any occasion or taste.
  • Beautiful scents: Not only do flowers look stunning, they also hold different fragrances. Can be delivered: If your loved one is a fair distance away or busy during the day, delivery is a great option for you. Sending flowers to their work office or front door is sure to brighten their day.

Do flowers count as gifts?

Flowers are a great birthday gift for anyone in your life. If you’re unsure what to give someone, flowers are a convenient and accessible choice that your loved one will always appreciate. They also can be a great addition to any other gifts you have in mind.

How to choose flowers for a birthday

With so many options, choosing the right flower bouquet can be a difficult task. If you know someone’s favourite flowers or colours, you can work together with a florist to create the perfect bunch! If you’re unsure, here are some popular flower options:

Roses

Nothing expresses romance more than a beautiful bouquet of roses. Red roses symbolise love, so these are better suited for a partner. If you’re looking to send a gift to a friend, yellow and pink shades are a good choice, signifying grace, gratitude and happiness.

Sunflowers

Sunflowers will give immediate joy to anyone who receives them. These radiant blooms are famous for their tall storks and vibrant petals. If you want to express loyalty, joy and unconditional love, sunflowers are the perfect birthday gift for that special someone!

Chrysanthemums

These wonderfully unique blooms are an excellent choice for gifting, either on their own or in a bouquet. Featuring hundreds of tiny petals, these blooms are bright and cheery, and symbolise love, happiness, longevity and well-being. They come in a variety of vibrant colours too!

Daisies

oming in a range of beautiful and vibrant shades, daisies are simple, yet a stunning choice for your happy birthday bouquet. They represent love, purity and new beginnings.

What colour flowers are best for birthdays?

When choosing birthday flowers, it’s best to opt for bright and vibrant shades such as red, pink, yellow, blue and purple. These colours are great for expressing joy, cheerfulness and love.
