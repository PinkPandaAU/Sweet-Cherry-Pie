How to order flowers: step by step

  1. Pick your flower bouquet and add it to your cart.
  2. Choose from local delivery and in-store pick up.
    • Same-day delivery orders must be placed by 11am.
    • In-person collections are available during opening hours of our City and Marion stores.
  3. If the flowers are being delivered, check we deliver to your area.
  4. Let us know if the flowers need to be delivered during business hours and if they’re safe to leave if no one is home.
  5. Head to the check-out page. Add in your contact, delivery and payment details.
  6. Once your order is complete, you’ll get a confirmation email.
  7. Then wait for your beautiful florals to arrive!

How do I care for fresh flowers?

If you just received a flower bouquet from someone special or decided to treat yourself, knowing how to care for fresh flowers is important to get the most out of your BloomBar bouquets!

  • Cut the end of the stems off with sharp scissors.
  • Place the flowers in a vase, filled with ¾ of fresh water.
  • Change the water and re-cut the stems every few days.
  • If you purchase flowers in a vase, they will already have water. All you need to do is follow the above steps the day after you receive the flowers.
  • If you purchase flowers in floral form, you need to refill the water daily so the flowers won’t dry out.
